CloudRader is an open-source initiative dedicated to building a connected ecosystem of independent, self-hostable services. We empower organizations and communities with tools that work perfectly on their own, but even better together.

---

## 🚀 The Ecosystem

We provide a collection of applications designed to solve everyday organizational needs without the complexity of traditional enterprise software.

!!! info "Reservium — Reservation System"
    Our flagship project. A practical tool for booking rooms, spaces, and shared resources. Supports SSO, multiple reservation types, and a clear, modern interface.

!!! info "Inventarium — Asset Tracking (Planned)"
    A lightweight system for tracking equipment and organizational assets. Designed for simplicity and ease of use.

---

## 🌈 Our Philosophy

*   **Self-Hostable by Default** — You own your data. Our apps are container-friendly and easy to deploy anywhere.
*   **Modular Architecture** — Each service is independent. Use only what you need, with no monolithic lock-in.
*   **Transparent Development** — Open code, open roadmaps, and open communication.
*   **Interoperability** — Designed to integrate seamlessly into your existing workflows.

---

## ☁️ Why “CloudRader”?

CloudRader is inspired by the idea of riding into the world of cloud technologies and conquering its complexity step by step. We build open-source tools, experiment with distributed systems, and try to make internal infrastructure less painful.

*Sometimes the cloud conquers us back—but that's just part of the deployment process.*
